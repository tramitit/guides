## Description
### What is it?
The Chilean Passport Service provides assistance to individuals who need to apply for or renew their passports. This service ensures that applicants can efficiently navigate the process and acquire their travel documents without unnecessary delays. The service covers all essential steps, including documentation, appointments, and submission procedures, to help Chilean citizens and residents obtain their passports seamlessly.

### Who needs it?
This service is ideal for Chilean citizens, both residents and those living abroad, who need to apply for a new passport or renew an existing one. Additionally, it's beneficial for individuals with permanent residency in Argentina seeking to travel internationally. Understanding the process, required documents, and timelines can significantly ease the application procedure for these individuals, ensuring they have a valid passport for their travels.

## Procedure

1. Check eligibility for a new or renewed Chilean passport.
2. Collect all the required documents.
3. Make an appointment at your nearest Civil Registry office.
4. Submit your application and required documents in person.
5. Pay the applicable fee for the passport application.
6. Wait for the processing of your application.
7. Receive notification when your passport is ready for collection.
8. Collect your passport from the Civil Registry office.

## Additional details

- Passport validity: Adults' passports are valid for 10 years, while passports for minors (under 18) are valid for 5 years.
- Fees vary depending on the type of passport and processing time.
- It's advisable to apply for or renew your passport well in advance of planned travel to avoid any delays.
- For more information, visit the official Civil Registry and Identification Service of Chile website.
