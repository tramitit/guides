## Description
### What is it?
A Tax Compliance Certificate in Ecuador is a mandatory document that certifies an individual or business has fulfilled all tax obligations. The certificate is needed for various legal processes, business transactions, and regulatory compliance within the country.

### Who needs it?
Ideal for foreign investors, entrepreneurs, and businesses operating in Ecuador. Anyone engaging in significant financial dealings, property transactions, or participating in governmental projects in Ecuador will find this certificate essential for legal and business activities.

## Procedure

1. Visit the official Ecuadorian tax authority website.
2. Sign up or log in to your account.
3. Submit an application for a Tax Compliance Certificate.
4. Provide all required documents as listed.
5. Await verification and processing.
6. Receive the Tax Compliance Certificate via email or by visiting the tax office.


## Required documents

- Valid ID (passport or local ID card) - Available at your local government office or embassy.
- Taxpayer Registration Number (RUC) - Obtainable from the Ecuadorian tax authority website.
- Proof of address - A utility bill or rental agreement works.
- Latest tax returns - Submit recent tax filings indicating compliance.
