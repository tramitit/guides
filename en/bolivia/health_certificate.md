## Description
### What is it?
A Health Certificate in Bolivia is an official document that verifies an individual's health status. It is essential for various purposes including employment, travel, and legal procedures. The certificate typically involves a medical examination and sometimes additional tests depending on the requirement.

### Who needs it?
An ideal customer for this service includes anyone who is planning to work, study, or travel internationally and needs to prove their health status. It is also necessary for pet owners who plan to bring their pets into Bolivia. Additionally, expats moving to Bolivia may require this certificate for legal and bureaucratic processes.

## Procedure

1. Visit a certified medical center or clinic in Bolivia.
2. Schedule an appointment for a medical examination.
3. Undergo the necessary medical tests and examinations as required.
4. Wait for the results and report preparation.
5. Collect the health certificate from the medical center once it is ready.


## Required documents

- Passport or national ID: Obtain from your respective national issuing authority.
- Medical History: Gather from your previous healthcare providers.
- Application Form: Fill out the form provided by the medical center.
